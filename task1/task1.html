About the Game

Rise of the Tomb Raider (2015) follows the iconic archaeologist, Lara Croft, as she embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secret of immortality. Set in the aftermath of her previous adventure, Lara is now driven to prove the existence of a legendary artifact known as the Divine Source, believed to grant eternal life. Her quest takes her to the remote, frozen wilderness of Siberia, where she faces hostile environments, an ancient and dangerous organization called Trinity, and her own personal struggles.

As players guide Lara through breathtaking landscapes and treacherous tombs, they will experience a thrilling mix of exploration, survival, and fast-paced combat. Rise of the Tomb Raider enhances the classic Tomb Raider formula with stunning visuals, intricate puzzle-solving, and dynamic action sequences, all while delving deeper into Lara’s growth as a character.

Discover hidden secrets, survive brutal conditions, and outwit enemies in this critically acclaimed action-adventure that pushes Lara to the limits of human endurance.

What's Special this time?

  • Stunning Visuals and Realistic Environments
    • Rise of the Tomb Raider delivers breathtaking graphics with meticulously crafted environments. Every detail, from the snow that realistically deforms underfoot to the lush, dynamic forests, has been designed to immerse players in Lara's world. Real-time lighting and advanced weather effects bring these environments to life, making them feel like an integral part of the experience.
  • Crafting and Survival Systems
    • The game emphasizes survival by integrating a comprehensive crafting system. Players can gather resources from the environment, such as wood, animal hides, and herbs, to create essential items like arrows, explosives, and healing supplies. This system enhances the sense of immersion and connection to the world, as players must use their surroundings to their advantage.
  • Challenging Tombs and Puzzles
    • Rise of the Tomb Raider features intricate, optional tombs with complex environmental puzzles. These tombs challenge players' problem-solving skills and offer rewarding experiences, such as unique abilities and rare artifacts. The design of these tombs encourages exploration and provides a sense of accomplishment for those who venture off the beaten path.
  • In-Game Rewards and Skill Upgrades
    • Progression in the game is driven by a detailed skill tree system. Players can enhance Lara’s abilities in areas like combat, stealth, and survival, tailoring her skills to their playstyle. In addition, exploring the world, completing missions, and solving puzzles yield valuable rewards, including new weapons, outfits, and tools, which contribute to an evolving gameplay experience.
  • Expedition Mode and Competitive Challenges
    • Expedition Mode allows players to replay levels with added challenges and modifiers, such as limited resources or tougher enemies. This mode also features leaderboards, enabling players to compete for high scores and achievements. Collectible cards that alter gameplay further enhance replayability, providing new ways to experience the game.
